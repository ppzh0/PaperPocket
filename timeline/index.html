Timeline

In this page, the accomplishments and goals of PaperPocket lie peacefully.

The Beginning of Our Non-Profit Journey

The concept for this non-profit website emerged just two days after the grade 9 final exams. We immediately began working on collecting high-quality soft copies of past exam papers while simultaneously focusing on the development of the website.

Enhancing Usability with a Language Update

In an effort to enhance usability, we updated the primary site language from English to Burmese, making it more accessible to our audience. However, select pages are still presented in English to maintain their accuracy and reliability.

Expanding Reach with Social Sharing Features

We introduced essential social features, such as sharing, to make it easier for users to connect and help us reach a wider audience.

Introducing a Timeline to Showcase Our Journey

We've introduced a timeline feature to visually showcase our journey, milestones, and progress. It provides a clear and engaging way for users to explore the evolution of our non-profit website.

Migrating to Bootstrap for a Simplified and Consistent User Interface

In our ongoing effort to improve the website's usability and visual consistency, we transitioned from manually coded CSS to Bootstrap. This update ensures a more responsive, streamlined design that works seamlessly across different devices. We also focused on simplifying the user interface to make it more accessible and intuitive for all users, enhancing the overall experience.

PaperPocket is finally on Facebook!!

We published a facebook page for better reach to our audience. Page's chatbox also handles users' questions and concerns. The Facebook page is named as the service: PaperPocket
